About Us

About Us

OUR MISSION

At CreativEdge, our goal is to provide the optimal product solution for your project in a timely and professional manner.

We fabricate parts via manufacturing methods such as flat cutting and engraving using CNC routers, CO2 lasers, and fiber lasers. We also offer limited thermoforming by way of flat sheet bending. Our custom projects have included watertight tanks, arcade game parts, machine guard covers, sneeze guards, acrylic partitions, metal substitute brackets, frosted acrylic counters, economically engineered acrylic brackets to replace OEM, and more.
